Frequently Asked Questions

~Is Foodies Bread really baked fresh each day?

At Tasty Bites , freshness is at the heart of everything we do, including our bread. Our artisan bakers start each morning crafting our breads from scratch using the finest ingredients. We believe in quality over quantity, which is why every loaf is baked fresh daily to ensure that you experience the true taste of our dedication.

~Do you bake breads containing animal fats or products?

Our breads are crafted using plant-based ingredients, and we do not use animal fats or products in our baking process. We prioritize using high-quality, wholesome ingredients to ensure that each loaf meets our standards of freshness and flavor.

~Can I order your products online?

Absolutely! You can now order our delicious dishes and treats online through our website. Simply browse our menu, place your order, and select either delivery or pickup. Enjoy the convenience of Tasty Bites delivered to your doorstep!

~When are you opening a shop near me?
